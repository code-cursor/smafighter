Update & Progress

March 13th, 2025

Update on Olivia's Journey – A Step Forward

With the grace of Allah and the incredible support from all of you, Abdullah and his mother, Fatema, successfully underwent their surgeries on March 7th without major complications. Today, we have a reason to be hopeful—Abdullah has been moved from the ICU to a cabin, and Fatema has been discharged from the hospital.


However, Abdullah’s journey is not over yet. Due to his small size, his muscles could not be stitched up completely—a common challenge in surgeries for infants. In about three months, he will need another surgery, which means additional financial support will be required.


We are deeply grateful to each and every one of you who has donated, prayed, and shared this appeal. Your generosity has brought them this far, and we ask you to continue standing by them in the next critical phase of their recovery.


Please keep Abdullah and Fatema in your prayers, and if possible, keep supporting and sharing their story.
